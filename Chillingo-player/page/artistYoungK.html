Young K

Kang Younghyun (강영현), or Young K (영케이) is the bassist and vocalist of the K-rock band DAY6 (deb. 2015). He is known to be the lead lyricist of the band, having written most of the songs in their discography. Young K is also part of DAY6's first subunit, DAY6 (Even of Day) along with members Wonpil and Dowoon. They debuted on August 31, 2020, with the mini album: The Book of Us: Gluon - Nothing Can Tear Us Apart.
